Why Oil?

The reasons for choosing heating oil for your home are numerous and can get a little technical. The short answers are:

  • Pricing - Oil prices are market driven and you have a choice of suppliers. With natural gas or electricity you're forced to deal with only one company.
  • Safety - Natural gas furnaces can malfunction and leak toxic carbon monoxide that may be difficult to notice. Oil furnaces will smoke before any harmful toxins are released.
  • Effeciency - Oil burns 400 degrees hotter than natural gas. Which means less time waiting for the house to warm up. It also means fewer hydrocarbons in the atmosphere. AND oil furnaces last twice as long as natural gas furnaces.

If you're thinking about switching away from oil get the facts first. Most people who do switch to natural gas end up regretting it. Get more information at Oregon Oil Heat.
